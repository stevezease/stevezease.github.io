Quiz/Poll/Survey

posted 5:30 PM Tuesday, February 2nd, 2016 (EST)

http://www.16personalities.com/free-personality-test

The quiz I took was called the myer briggs personality quiz. The quiz is designed to tell you which personality type of the sixteen you are. After taking the quiz I found out that I was an INTP. It is important to note that I have completed a multitude of myers briggs quizzes in the past and found that some have been unbearably long and repetitive. Although I the increase in questions would probably increase accuracy, I also felt that the accuracy of my responses also decreased as the quiz progressed. 3% of people who took the quiz had the same results as me: “INTP”. I feel quite unique as intp is one of the rarer personality types on the myer brigg scale. I found it interesting that the statistical breakup of the two subgroups (such as Introverted vs Extroverted or Perceiving vs Judgmental) were largely close to 50 50. There was one large exception when it came to intuition vs sensing where the break up was now 25 to 75. One area of concern is that various myers briggs tests used various types of answer types. We once again come back to the tradeoff between the length of the quiz vs accuracy. Some myers briggs tests used a scroller from strongly agree to strongly disagree. Others used straight yes or no answer options. I liked the straight yes or no, but felt that the scroller offered a more accurate response as many of the questions did not have definite yes or no answers. I found that from the statistics that I was using, rather than a concrete percentage, the statistics gave a range for each personality.
